USAA Banking Pitch

While interning at Blue Sky Innovations, a startup in Chapel Hill, my team developed a cohesive presentation to pitch our idea for leveraging chatbots to engage banking customers in financial planning for USAA Banking to the EVP of USAA and her team.

Team Structure

My team consisted of six students, each possessing their own specialized skillsets. Alongside myself, two others primarily focused on user research and prototyping using Figma. The remaining members undertook tasks such as sketching and illustrating Atlas, our proposed avatar, while developers worked on mapping out the logic of the chatbot.

Skills Utilized

We conducted user research primarily through a survey distributed to members of the Reserve Officers' Training Corps (ROTC) at UNC-Chapel Hill. Our prototyping was carried out using Figma, creating versions of an educational chatbot for USAA's website and an HR portal exclusively for USAA Banking employees. In addition to my design skills, I contributed to the project management, storytelling, public speaking, and ideation aspects of the project.
