Taste Buddy Pitch

For my capstone in Information Science at UNC-Chapel Hill, I worked with a team of students to research and design Taste Buddy, a grocery shopping application aimed at helping college students reduce food waste, save money, and shop more efficiently. At the end of the semester, we presented our findings, mockups, and argued for the necessity of Taste Buddy in the current market.

Team Structure

Our team of 3 students had one semester to respond to the prompt “How might we leverage AI to improve the lives of college students?” Each member of our team fulfilled a different role, that being a project manager, lead designer, and document manager.

Skills Utilized

As the lead designer for this project, I was responsible for creating wireframes and prototypes for Taste Buddy. Additionally, I facilitated multiple focus groups involving a total of 30 college students as part of our initial research phase for the application.
